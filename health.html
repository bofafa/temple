Restore Balance in Sichuan

Journey through Mount Qingcheng — a Taoist sanctuary where nature and spirit unite for health and harmony.

Mount Qingcheng 青城山

City:Dujiangyan, Sichuan, China
Deity:Laozi (Lao Tzu) & various Taoist Immortals
Religion:Taoism (Daoism)
Address:Mount Qingcheng Scenic Area, Dujiangyan
Opening Hours:8:00 AM - 6:00 PM daily

A sacred mountain rich in natural energy and Taoist tradition. Known as the birthplace of Chinese Taoism, it's an ideal retreat to reflect, reset, and cultivate both body and soul.

How to Make a Wish

  1. Begin your hike slowly, breathing deeply as you walk the peaceful forest path.
  2. At a Taoist shrine, bow three times and focus your intention on health and renewal.
  3. Drink mountain spring water or meditate by a stream to absorb its healing Qi (氣).
  4. Write your wish for vitality or healing and place it at a prayer altar or in a wooden box if available.

Nearby Attractions

  • Dujiangyan Irrigation System – an ancient waterworks still in use today.
  • Back Mountain Trail – for forest bathing and panoramic views.

Travel Tips

  • Take a high-speed train from Chengdu to Qingchengshan Station (~30 min), then transfer by bus or taxi.
  • Dress comfortably for nature walks; bring water and mosquito spray in warmer months.
  • Quiet areas are best visited in the early morning for peaceful meditation.
