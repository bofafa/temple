💰 Find Fortune in Tokyo

Gotokuji Temple — the home of the lucky beckoning cat and Japan’s symbol of prosperity.

Gotokuji Temple (豪徳寺) – Tokyo, Japan

  • City: Tokyo (Setagaya Ward), Japan
  • Deity: Maneki Neko (Beckoning Cat for wealth)
  • Address: 2-24-7 Gotokuji, Setagaya City, Tokyo
  • Opening Hours: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Temple History: Founded during the Edo period (1480s), this temple is said to be the birthplace of the Maneki Neko legend — where a lucky cat saved a samurai from a lightning storm, bringing prosperity to the temple.

🧧 How to Make a Wish

  • 🐱 Buy a Maneki Neko figurine from the temple shop.
  • ✍️ Write your financial wish on the bottom of the cat.
  • ⛩️ Offer it at the altar or take it home for prosperity.

📍 Nearby Attractions

  • Shimo-Takaido Shopping Street: Experience old-school Tokyo with local eats and shops.
  • Shimokitazawa: Trendy area for thrift shops, music, and indie cafés.

🧳 Travel Tips

  • 🍁 Best time to visit: Autumn (Oct–Nov) for beautiful maple leaves and mild weather.
  • 🎎 This temple is peaceful and less touristy — a great photo spot with hundreds of cat statues!
