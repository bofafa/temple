💼 Boost Your Career in Bangkok

Visit the Erawan Shrine — a powerful place to ask for career success and stability.

Erawan Shrine (ศาลท้าวมหาพรหม) – Bangkok, Thailand

  • City: Bangkok, Thailand
  • Deity: Phra Phrom (Thai Brahma – creation & stability)
  • Address: Ratchadamri Road, near CentralWorld Mall
  • Opening Hours: 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM

Temple History: Built in 1956 to counteract bad luck at a construction site, Erawan Shrine has become one of Bangkok’s most iconic spiritual sites for career advancement and wishes for stability in life.

🙏 How to Make a Wish

  • 🕯️ Offer incense, candles, jasmine garlands, and wooden elephant statues.
  • 🛕 Pray at all four faces of Phra Phrom — for career, health, wealth, and relationships.
  • 🎉 If your wish comes true, return with a dancer performance or additional offerings as thanks.

📍 Nearby Attractions

  • CentralWorld & Siam Paragon: Luxury malls for shopping and local eats.
  • Bangkok Art & Culture Centre: A cultural hotspot for exhibitions and events.

🧳 Travel Tips

  • 📅 Best time: Weekday mornings — less crowded and peaceful for prayers.
  • 🎓 Many Thai workers come here during job transitions or new business launches.
  • 🚇 Easily accessible by BTS Skytrain (Chit Lom Station).
