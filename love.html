💕 Find Love in Taipei

Visit the Yue Lao Shrine at Xia-Hai City God Temple — one of Asia’s most legendary matchmaking spots.

Xia-Hai City God Temple (台北霞海城隍廟)

  • City: Taipei, Taiwan
  • Deity: Yue Lao (God of Marriage & Love)
  • Address: No. 61, Dihua St, Datong District, Taipei
  • Opening Hours: 6:16 AM – 7:47 PM daily

Temple History: Built in 1859, this small yet famous temple draws thousands of visitors seeking love. Known to have one of the most effective Yue Lao statues in the world, it sits in the heart of historic Dadaocheng.

🧧 How to Make a Wish

  • 🎁 Get a red string and incense from the temple booth.
  • 🙏 State your name, birthday, and partner preferences to Yue Lao.
  • 🔗 Tie the red string to your wallet or phone afterward.

📍 Nearby Attractions

  • Dadaocheng Old Street: A lovely heritage district with tea houses and souvenir shops.
  • Ningxia Night Market: Try local favorites like oyster omelets and taro balls.

🧳 Travel Tips

  • 📅 Best time to visit: Valentine’s Day (Feb 14) or Qixi Festival (Chinese Valentine’s Day).
  • 🌄 Morning hours are most peaceful for making sincere wishes.
